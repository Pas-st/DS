The idea for this game came to me when I saw a video about black holes, just in the Moment when they spoke about jerks, I formed the Idea about one spaceship that flew in space and is near zero gravity and zero friction. Then I started thinking about the goal: “absolve the level best!” With the best time, the best health remaining, the lowest number of moves.
I this Game you’re going to make a Scratch (https://scratch.mit.edu/) like building, but every block that is placed will affect the spaceship, so when “black hole exists” (like when green-flag-clicked in scratch) those blocks are executed after each other, in that order you placed them.
You Complete the level by going through a black hole, the faster you complete the better your time.

Inspired by „The Farmer was replaced“:
Program and optimize a drone to automate a farm and watch it do the work for you. Collect resources to unlock better technology and become the most efficient farmer in the world. Improve your problem solving and coding skills.
