The idea for this game came to me when I saw a video about black holes, just in the Moment when they spoke about jerks, I formed the Idea about one spaceship that flew in space and is near zero gravity and zero friction. Then I started thinking about the goal: “absolve the level best!” With the best time, the best health remaining, the lowest number of moves.
I this Game you’re going to make a scratch like building, but every block that is placed will affect the spaceship, so when “black hole exists” (like when green-flag-clicked in scratch) those blocks are executed after each other, in that order you placed them.
You Complete the level by going through a black hole, the faster you complete the better your time.
